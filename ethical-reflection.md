# Ethical Dilemma Reflection

The ethical dilemma I examined involved misuse of administrative privileges and lack of transparency in handling system access.

Through analyzing this case, I realized that ethical challenges in IT are rarely technical problems; they are character tests.

My view is that transparency and accountability must take priority over reputation management. While organizations may fear public exposure, ethical concealment often leads to long-term damage.  

If faced with a similar situation in my future work, I would:

  + Immediately restrict inappropriate access.

  + Conduct an objective review.

  + Communicate transparently with stakeholders.

  + Strengthen internal controls.

This exercise deepened my understanding that technical authority carries moral responsibility. Ethical awareness must be proactive, not reactive.
